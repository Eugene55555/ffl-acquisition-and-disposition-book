# Introducing FFL — Acquisition & Disposition

This is the companion blog for the *FFL: Acquisition and Disposition* book.
The book walks through how firearms-related acquisitions and dispositions
actually work — the paperwork, the timelines, and the traps people fall into.

## What you will find here

- Chapter notes and clarifications that did not fit in the print version.
- Real-world examples of acquisition and disposition scenarios.
- Updates when regulations change.

## A note on accuracy

Rules vary by jurisdiction and change often. Treat posts here as educational
context, not legal advice. Always verify against the current official sources
before acting.

More posts are coming as the book develops.
